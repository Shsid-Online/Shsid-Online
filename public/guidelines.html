Community guidelines

Draft for discussion with school leadership. Not a legal instrument.

  • Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and targeted bullying are not allowed.
  • Share accurate information in academic contexts; clearly label opinions and jokes.
  • Respect privacy: do not post photos or names of others without consent.
  • School staff and designated admins may review reports and verification submissions.
  • Anonymous posts still belong to you; abuse of anonymity can lead to moderation action.
  • If you see something unsafe, use in-app reporting or speak to a trusted adult immediately.
