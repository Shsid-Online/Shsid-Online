Privacy overview

High-level summary for the prototype. Replace with counsel-approved policy before production.

  • Account data (email, profile fields, verification artifacts) is stored to operate the service and enforce school-only access.
  • Moderators may view reported content and, where allowed by policy, deanonymize authors for safety investigations.
  • Usage may be logged for security and audit purposes (sign-ins, moderation actions).
  • Students and families should receive a formal privacy notice before real rollout.
  • Deletion and export processes must be defined before storing identifiable minor data at scale.
